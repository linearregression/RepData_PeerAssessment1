Strategy to replace missing data. Try out replace missing data with mean and median per corresponding interval, then compare changes. Since mean is senstive to extreme values, try out median.

fitdata2<- read.csv(file='activity.csv', header=TRUE, stringsAsFactor=FALSE, na.strings = "NA", colClasses=c("integer", "Date", "integer"))
